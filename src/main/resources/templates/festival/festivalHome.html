About FPT Festival 2025

FPT Festival 2025 is the biggest cultural event at FPT University, bringing together young talents, creative ideas and innovative spirit. The event provides opportunities for networking, learning and personal development for all students. With over 25 diverse activities from technology exhibitions, intensive workshops, to cultural and artistic activities, FPT Festival 2025 promises to be an unforgettable experience for all participants. We are committed to creating a creative space where every idea is respected and developed.

Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about FPT Festival 2025

How to register for the festival?
You can register online through our website or contact the organizing committee directly for assistance.
Who can participate in FPT Festival?
All FPT University students can participate. Some activities may require advance registration.
What activities does the festival include?
The festival includes technology exhibitions, intensive workshops, cultural and artistic activities, networking and many other exciting activities.
Do I need to bring anything special?
Just bring your student ID and a ready-to-participate spirit. Some workshops may require a personal laptop.
Is there a participation fee?
Participation in the festival is completely free for all FPT University students.
How to contact the organizing committee?
You can contact us via email: festival@fpt.edu.vn or call hotline: 0123-456-789.
